In the first semester of B.S. degree, a student is expected to take 4 subjects. The grades for each subject can be $S (10 \text{points}), A (9 \text{points}), B (8 \text{points}), C (7 \text{points}), D (6 \text{points}), E (5 \text{points})$ and $F \text{(Fail)}$. As per the rules, an enrolled student should obtain a minimum grade $E$ in each subject to get promoted to the next semester. What is the total number of possible ways in which a student can get promoted$?$
 
  
::: {.callout-note title="Hint" collapse=true}



:::

::: {.callout-note title="Answer" collapse=true}
1296
   
:::
