A family has six children, three boys and three girls. Assuming all birth orders are equally likely, what is the probability that the three eldest children are girls?

::: {.callout-note title='Answer' collapse=true}

0.05

:::

::: {.callout-note title='Solution' collapse=true}

Label the boys $B_1, B_2, B_3$ and the girls $G_1, G_2, G_3$. Since all orders are equally likely, we have:

$$
\cfrac{3! \times 3!}{6!} = \cfrac{1}{20}
$$

:::
