Suppose person $X$ speaks truth with a probability of $3/5$ and  person $Y$ speaks  truth with a probability of $5/8$. If you ask both of them their opinions on a story, what is the probability that their opinions will be contradictory$?$ Enter the answer correct to two decimal places. 
 
  
::: {.callout-note title="Hint" collapse=true}



:::

::: {.callout-note title="Answer" collapse=true}
0.48
   
:::
