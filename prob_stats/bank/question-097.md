If the mean of the observations $2a, 10, 3a,50$ and $40$ is $30,$ then find the median of the dataset.
 
  
::: {.callout-note title="Hint" collapse=true}
Find the value of $a$ first by using the formula of mean and then find all the observations to compute median.


:::

::: {.callout-note title="Answer" collapse=true}
30
   
:::
