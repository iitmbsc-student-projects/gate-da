::: {.callout-note title="Soluton" collapse=true}

The first fold gives us a rectangle of shape 10cm x 8 cm. The second fold gives us a rectangle of shape 5cm x 8 cm. The third and final fold results in a rectangle of shape 5cm x 4cm. Its perimeter is 18 cm.
