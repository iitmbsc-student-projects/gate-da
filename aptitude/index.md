A non-exhaustive syllabus for this section:

- English comprehension
- Logical thinking
- Visual and spatial reasoning
- Elementary mathematics
  - basic number theory
  - algebra
  - geometry
  - trigonometry
  - sets and functions
  - basic probability
  - basic statistics
  - profit and loss

There are ten questions from GA every year. Five of these questions carry one mark each and the rest two marks. A total of 15 marks is allocated to GA. All the questions that are in this bank are drawn from previous year GATE papers, particularly CS, the nearest paper to DA.
