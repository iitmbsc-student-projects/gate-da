::: {.callout-note title="Soluton" collapse=true}

A bit of angle chasing gives us the following:

![](img-4.png)

Now, using a bit of trigonometry, we see the following:

$$
PL = \cfrac{2}{\sin(\theta)}, PN = 2 \sin(\theta)
$$

Therefore, the area of the rectangle $PLMN$ is $4$.
