Which one of the following options is correct for the given data in the table?

| Iteration ($i$) | Input ($I$) | Output ($X$) | Output ($Y$) |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| 0 | 20 | 20 | 20 |
| 1 | -4 | 16 | -80 |
| 2 | 10 | 26 | -800 |
| 3 | 15 | 41 | -12000 |

- [ ] $X(i) = X(i-1) + I(i); \quad Y(i) = Y(i-1)I(i); \quad i > 0$

- [ ] $X(i) = X(i-1)I(i); \quad Y(i) = Y(i-1) + I(i); \quad i > 0$

- [ ] $X(i) = X(i-1)I(i); \quad Y(i) = Y(i-1)I(i); \quad i > 0$

- [ ] $X(i) = X(i-1) + I(i); \quad Y(i) = Y(i-1)I(i-1); \quad i > 0$

::: {.callout-note title="Answer" collapse=true}
