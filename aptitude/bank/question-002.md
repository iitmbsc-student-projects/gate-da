Bird : Nest :: Bee : ______

- [ ] Kennel
- [ ] Hammock
- [ ] Hive
- [ ] Lair

::: {.callout-note title="Answer" collapse=true}

- [ ] Kennel
- [ ] Hammock
- [x] Hive
- [ ] Lair

:::

::: {.callout-note title="Soluton" collapse=true}

$X : Y$ is equivalent to $X$ lives in $Y$ or $Y$ is the home of $X$. 

Bees live in hives.

:::
